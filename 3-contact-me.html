The Story

Named after a rhythm game's artwork theme, the Digi-Groove Controller allows the user to play machine-dancing with their hands instead of just their feet.

Back in 2015, I saw the coolest arcade cabinet. It was a rhythm dance game called "Digi-Groove." What made it so awesome was that you could play with hands instead of feet! I fell for it, and spent a lot of time and quarters there. I liked it so much that I decided I wanted one too. So I built one.

It was quite the process. Not knowing much about what to do, I recruited friends and professionals who knew something about hardware and software. With the help of two dear friends, we created two "Digi-Groove Controllers". These were far more portable than a cabinet and really helped cut down the building costs.
